Good to know

Frequently asked questions

The things parents usually want to clear up before we start. Still not sure about something? Just ask.

How and where do the lessons take place?+

All lessons are online over Zoom, using a shared interactive whiteboard so your child and I can work through problems together in real time, just like sitting side by side, from the comfort of home.

Will my child get homework, and how will I know how they're doing?+

Yes, I set homework after each session to consolidate what we've covered, and I'll send you a short progress update so you always know how your child is getting on and what we're working towards.

What does the free first session involve?+

The first session is completely free with no commitment. We'll get to know each other, talk through your child's goals and any tricky topics, and make sure it's a good fit before booking anything further.

Which exam boards and subjects do you cover?+

I tutor GCSE Maths & Physics and A-Level Maths & Further Maths across AQA, Edexcel and OCR. Lessons are always tailored to your child's specification and exam board.

How does payment work?+

Sessions are paid per hour at the rates listed above, with no contract and no joining fee. If something comes up, just message me ahead of the lesson and we'll move it to another slot.

Good to know

GCSE Physics questions

The things parents usually want to know before we start.

Do you cover combined science and triple physics?+

Yes. Whether your child takes Physics as part of Combined Science (Trilogy) or as a separate triple-science GCSE, I cover the full content for their tier and exam board.

Can you help with the required practicals and equations?+

Definitely. The required practicals and the physics equations are where a lot of marks are won or lost. We practise the experiments, the equations and the longer-answer questions so your child knows exactly what examiners are looking for.

Which GCSE Physics exam boards do you cover?+

I tutor GCSE Physics across AQA, Edexcel and OCR. Every session follows your child's exact specification, so we practise the question styles and mark schemes for their board.

Is the first session really free?+

Yes. The first session is completely free with no commitment. We look at the topics your child finds hardest and make sure it's a good fit before booking anything further.

How do payment and cancellations work?+

Sessions are £25 per hour, paid per session. Please give at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule. Cancellations within 24 hours are charged in full.

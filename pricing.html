Good to know

Pricing questions

The things parents usually want to clear up before booking.

Is the first session really free?+

Yes. The first session is completely free with no commitment. We get to know each other, look at the topics your child finds hardest, and make sure it's a good fit before you book anything further.

Do I pay upfront, or is there a contract?+

Neither. You pay per session at the rate for your child's subject, and there are no joining fees, no cancellation fees and no contract. You can book as many or as few sessions as you like.

Why is Further Maths more than the other subjects?+

A-Level Further Maths covers more advanced, specialist material on top of the standard A-Level, so it takes more preparation per session. That's reflected in the £40 per hour rate.

Do I need to book a block of sessions?+

No. Pricing is a simple flat rate per session, so there's no block to buy upfront. Most families settle into a regular weekly slot, but you're free to book around exams, mocks or holidays as it suits your child.

Good to know

GCSE Maths questions

The things parents usually want to know before we start.

Do you teach both foundation and higher tier?+

Yes. I tutor both foundation (grades 1–5) and higher tier (grades 4–9). If you're not sure which tier suits your child best, we can talk it through in the free first session and aim for the grade they need.

Which GCSE Maths exam boards do you cover?+

I tutor GCSE Maths across AQA, Edexcel and OCR. Every session follows your child's exact specification, so we practise the question styles and mark schemes for their board.

My child has lost confidence in maths — can you help?+

Absolutely. A lot of my students start out anxious about maths. We go back to fill the gaps that are tripping them up, then build from there with real exam questions until things start to click and their confidence grows.

Is the first session really free?+

Yes. The first session is completely free with no commitment. We get to know each other, look at the topics your child finds hardest, and make sure it's a good fit before booking anything further.

How does payment work?+

Sessions are £25 per hour, paid per session, with no contract and no joining fee. If something comes up, just message me ahead of the lesson and we'll move it to another slot.

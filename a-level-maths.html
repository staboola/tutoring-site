Good to know

A-Level Maths questions

The things parents usually want to know before we start.

Which A-Level Maths exam boards do you cover?+

I tutor A-Level Maths across AQA, Edexcel and OCR (including OCR MEI). Every session follows your child's exact specification, so we practise the question styles and mark schemes for their board.

What does an A-Level Maths session look like?+

We work through real exam questions and past papers together on a shared online whiteboard. I show your child how to read what each question is actually asking, then they practise it until the method sticks.

Is the first session really free?+

Yes. The first session is completely free with no commitment. We get to know each other, look at the topics your child finds hardest, and make sure it's a good fit before booking anything further.

Will my child get homework and progress updates?+

Yes. I set homework after each session to lock in what we've covered, and I send you a short progress update so you always know how your child is getting on.

How does payment work?+

Sessions are £30 per hour, paid per session, with no contract and no joining fee. If something comes up, just message me ahead of the lesson and we'll move it to another slot.
